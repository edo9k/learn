This a base theme for a more Brutalist WordPress.

No fancy shit. Just good, old, robust, light HTML.

The ideia behind this is the following...

Focus on the content. Not on the form.

This theme does not come with any Javascript, or even CSS.

But it does come with a good structural HTML base, into which you can later add your CSS. And, sparingly, Javascript.

Be mindful that highspeed internet access is not widespread.

Neither are a bunch of features in the latest Chromes, Firefoxes, Safaris and what-not.

This theme aims to:

  • Cut the bullshit.
  • Be light on resources.
  • Be accessible with any device and bandwidth.
  • Work even in ancient browsers.
